► What is your usual deadline for commissions?
I usually finish within 1–2 weeks, depending on my schedule. I'll keep you posted if anything comes up, but I'm typically quick at hitting deadlines once they're set! That said, if you're not in a rush and are okay with a flexible timeline, it gives me more room to experiment and add extra polish. I'm reliable either way!
► What setup do you use to draw?
A HUION H640P.. Yes I know it's a pretty old model.. but it still works! I love my little war machine! And I use a laptop also.
► Can I repost your work/use in an edit?
To be honest, I think it's inevitable for other people to use your work if it ever gathers attention; it's kind of the nature of social media and it's a common problem, unfortunately. So, I'll say I don't mind you guys reposting it, so long as you do actually credit me. It's a win for me either way since it's a way my clout gets spread out lol.
► Are you open to connecting with other artists and creatives?
Absolutely! I'd love to connect with people who are passionate about art and creativity. I'm looking for folks who can give constructive feedback, share ideas, and maybe collaborate or just vibe over creative stuff. I'm pretty approachable, but heads up—I'm often busy, so I might not be super active every day. Still, if you need someone to bounce ideas off of or just want to share your work, feel free to reach out! ^^
